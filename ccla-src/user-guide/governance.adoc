== Overview

In Cloudclapp we have two main roles *Admin* and *Manager* , we will explain bellow how to get those roles and how to define permissions/rights for them:

To get an Admin account you have to register in Cloudclapp by clinking on *Get Started* in login page then fill the fom  and click on *Continue*

image:login_page.png[alt=Governance Menu, width=600px]
  
image:register_page.png[alt=Governance Menu, width=600px]
  
image:verify_email_popup.png[alt=Governance Menu, width=600px]
  
You will recieve an email contains a link to activate your account , clik on it and your acoount will be activated
  
image:registration_email_content.png[alt=Governance Menu, width=600px]
image:activated_account.png[alt=Governance Menu, width=600px]

=> Congratulation your *Admin* account is ready to use !!

Now to get a *Manager* account you have to *invite it*  , you can check the :ref:Invit Users https://github.com/ubiqube/docs/blob/master/ccla-src/user-guide/governance.adoc#invit-users for more details
